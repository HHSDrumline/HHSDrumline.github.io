About the Band Boosters Program

Band is an expensive activity. Instruments, equipment, uniforms and travel all cost a great deal of money. We have an active group of parents who work continually to supplement the band budget and to help provide the band with chaperones for travel and assistance with various performances and activities.

There are many areas in which parents can get involved in the band program. We need Band Booster Officers (President, Vice-President, Secretary, and Treasurer). We also need volunteers for various areas such as uniforms, fundraising, hospitality, PR/communication, chaperones, decorations, concerts and our talent show.

The Band Boosters meet quarterly in conjunction with a band activity, concert, or program.
